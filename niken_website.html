Converse Welcomes Baby
Keem to Its Creative
Community

What to know
  • Keem, a 21-year-old artist and producer, expands Converse's community of
    progressive creative voices
  • Keem will bring his creative vision to the Chuck Taylor All Star.
  • Keem will work with converse to create unique experiences with the Converse
    All Star community.

21-year-old artist and producer, Baby Keem has joined Converse's creative community. He is the latest member of an expanding roster comprised of progressive voices in music, sport and culture, some of whom include Tyler, The Creator, Natasha Cloud, pgLang, Shai Gilgeous-Alxander and Alexis Sablone.

With Converse, Keem will explore multiple dimensions of the iconic Chuck Taylor All Star. In addition, he will partner with Converse on unique experiences with the Converse All Stars, the brand's creator community, giving emerging talent access to mentorship, experiences and opportunities.
